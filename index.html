How To Play

1. You have 1 Minute to Find Buried Treasures in the Map.

2. There are 4 treasure spots located on the map.

3. Swipe mouse over the map and find the buried treasure

4. If you are closer to hunt the treasure. Temprature becomes more warmer.

5. If you are far from the treasure. Temparature becomes more colder.

6. Find all buried treasures within a minute for a win .

7. You will hear a sound effect each the treaure is found .
